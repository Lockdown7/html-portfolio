The Best Movies According to Alysha

My top 3 movies of all-time

Transformers Dark of the Moon

I love cars and robots. I really liked the animations of the transformers. My favorite characters are Bumblebee and Soundwave.

Fast Five

I loved everything in this movie. The cars, the action, and the story of the movie. I still can't decide if I like F4 more than F5.

Big Hero 6

I find the movie heartouching and inspiring. I really like the storyline and the robot Baymax.
